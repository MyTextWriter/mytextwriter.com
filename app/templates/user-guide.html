How It Works

MyTexWriter provides a straightforward process for secure text sharing:

1. Generate a Link

Write or paste your text and select "Generate Link". The system will instantly create a secure, unique link for your session.

2. Share the Link

Copy and share the generated link through your preferred communication channel - email, messaging app, or direct message.

3. Collaborate in Real-Time

Recipients can instantly access and edit the text through the shared link, enabling seamless real-time collaboration.

Important Note:

The link will regenerate if the original page is refreshed, and the previous link will become inactive.

FAQs

  • Do I need an account to use MyTexWriter? No, an account is not required to use MyTexWriter. You can start generating and sharing links immediately. In the future, we may introduce account features for added functionality.
  • Is my text secure? Yes, your text is only accessible through the unique link you generate and share. No one can access it without the link.
  • Can the link expire? Yes, each link expires once the page is refreshed by the starter (the user who generates the link) or closed. By default, links expire after 30 minutes.
  • Can multiple devices access the link? Yes, up to three devices can be connected to the same link, with a maximum of two devices from the same link at a time.
  • Is my text saved? No, MyTexWriter operates in real-time collaboration, and no text is saved to our servers. Once the session ends, your text is no longer accessible.
  • Can I edit text offline? No, text can only be edited when the page is open and connected via the unique link. Once the starter connection is lost or the starter page is closed, the session ends.
  • Can I integrate MyTexWriter with other platforms? Currently, MyTexWriter operates as a standalone tool, but we are looking into future integrations with popular productivity platforms and applications.
